## Shipping Policy

GigaCorp ships to over 40 countries, including the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, Australia, and India. Domestic orders (within the United States) are delivered within 3-5 business days using Standard Shipping, or 1-2 business days using Express Shipping. International orders, including shipments to India, typically arrive within 7-14 business days. Standard international shipping costs a flat rate of $19.99, and shipping to India specifically costs $24.99 due to customs handling fees. Orders over $150 qualify for free Standard Shipping within the United States. GigaCorp does not currently ship to P.O. boxes or military APO/FPO addresses.

## Return Process

GigaCorp accepts returns within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund, provided the item is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation. To start a return, log into your GigaCorp account, navigate to "Order History," select the item, and click "Request Return." A prepaid return shipping label will be emailed within 24 hours for domestic orders. International returns require the customer to cover return shipping costs, which are non-refundable. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse. Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged. Defective or damaged items can be returned within 60 days for a full refund or replacement, no questions asked.

## Business Hours

GigaCorp's customer support team is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time, and Saturday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM Eastern Time. We are closed on Sundays and major U.S. holidays, including New Year's Day, Independence Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. Live chat support, available on our website, operates during the same hours. Email support (support@gigacorp.example) is monitored 24/7, with a guaranteed response time of within 24 hours on business days. Our warehouse and fulfillment centers process orders Monday through Saturday, excluding holidays.

## Service Tiers

GigaCorp offers three membership tiers: Basic, Plus, and Premium.
Basic (free): Standard shipping rates apply, standard 30-day return window, email support only.
Plus ($9.99/month or $99/year): Free Standard Shipping on all domestic orders regardless of order size, extended 45-day return window, priority email support with a 12-hour response guarantee, and access to live chat support.
Premium ($19.99/month or $199/year): Free Express Shipping on domestic orders and discounted international shipping (50% off), extended 60-day return window, 24/7 priority phone and chat support, and early access to sales and new product launches. Premium members also receive a dedicated account manager for orders over $500.
Members can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel their tier at any time from the "Membership" section of their account settings; changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Order Tracking

Once an order ships, customers receive a tracking number via email and SMS (if opted in). Tracking information can also be viewed under "Order History" in the GigaCorp account portal. It typically takes 24-48 hours after shipment for tracking information to update with the carrier. If a tracking number shows no movement for more than 5 business days, customers should contact support for investigation.

## Payment and Billing

GigaCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and GigaCorp Store Credit. Orders are charged at the time of purchase, not at the time of shipment. Subscription tier charges (Plus and Premium) are billed automatically on the monthly or annual anniversary of signup. Failed payments result in a 3-day grace period before the account tier reverts to Basic.
